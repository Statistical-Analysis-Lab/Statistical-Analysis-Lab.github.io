## Normal Distribution


$$
\bar{x} = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\frac{x}{\sigma \sqrt{2\pi}}\exp\left[-\frac{\left(x-\mu\right){2}}{2\sigma{2}}\right]
$$

## Moment Generate Function

$$
M_{X}\left(t\right) = E\left[e^{tX}\right], \quad t\in\mathbb{R}
$$
